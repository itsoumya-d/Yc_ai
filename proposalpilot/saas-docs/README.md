# ProposalPilot

**Win more deals, write fewer proposals.**

ProposalPilot is an AI-powered proposal and SOW generation platform built for agencies and consultancies. It transforms hours of proposal writing into minutes of intelligent drafting, learning your agency's voice, tracking what wins deals, and helping you close faster.

---

## The Problem

Every agency and consultancy faces the same brutal math: proposals take 8-15 hours to write, and nearly half of them lose. That means a mid-size agency writing 10 proposals per month burns 80-150 hours on documents that have a coin-flip chance of converting. Senior staff get pulled from billable client work. Templates get stale. Pricing is guesswork. And nobody tracks why proposals win or lose.

The result: agencies limit how many deals they pursue, leaving revenue on the table.

## The Solution

ProposalPilot uses AI to generate professional, customized proposals and Statements of Work in minutes. Feed it a client brief, select from your past winning proposals, and choose services from your catalog. ProposalPilot drafts a complete proposal with scope, timeline, pricing breakdowns, terms, and team recommendations -- all in your agency's voice and brand.

But generation is just the start. ProposalPilot tracks every proposal's lifecycle: who opened it, which sections they read, how long they spent on pricing, whether they shared it internally. Over time, it builds a win-rate intelligence layer that reveals which pricing models, scope structures, and terms correlate with winning deals -- and which kill them.

It is the difference between sending 5 proposals a week and 20 -- with better win rates on each one.

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors

| Product | Approach | Pricing | Weakness vs ProposalPilot |
| ------- | -------- | ------- | ------------------------- |
| **Proposify** | Template-based proposals with e-sign | $49/user/mo | No AI generation, no win-rate analytics, templates require manual population |
| **PandaDoc** | Document automation + e-sign | $35/user/mo | Broad horizontal tool (not agency-specific), AI is bolt-on not native, pricing tables are rigid |
| **Qwilr** | Web-based interactive proposals | $35/user/mo | Beautiful but manual creation, no AI drafting, limited analytics beyond open tracking |
| **Better Proposals** | Template library with tracking | $19/user/mo | No AI generation, basic analytics, limited CRM integrations, SMB-focused |
| **Responsive (RFPIO)** | RFP response automation | Enterprise pricing | Enterprise-only, RFP-focused (not proactive proposals), complex setup |

### Indirect Competitors

| Product | Overlap | Differentiation |
| ------- | ------- | --------------- |
| **Google Docs + ChatGPT** | Manual AI-assisted writing | No proposal-specific structure, no tracking, no analytics, no e-sign |
| **Canva** | Visual proposal design | Design tool, not a proposal workflow; no pricing tables, tracking, or AI drafting |
| **HubSpot Quotes** | CRM-native quoting | Limited to HubSpot ecosystem, basic quote format, no rich proposals or SOWs |

### Competitive Moat

1. **AI-native generation** -- Competitors bolt on AI features to template libraries; ProposalPilot is built from day one around generation, learning, and optimization
2. **Win-rate intelligence** -- No competitor provides analytics that correlate proposal attributes (pricing model, scope detail, timeline, terms) with win/loss outcomes
3. **Agency-specific domain model** -- Trained on agency proposal structures, pricing strategies (fixed, T&M, value-based, retainer), and SOW frameworks rather than generic business documents
4. **Compounding data advantage** -- Every proposal created and every outcome tracked improves the AI's ability to suggest winning structures, pricing, and language
5. **Voice learning** -- ProposalPilot learns each agency's tone, terminology, and preferred structures, making outputs increasingly indistinguishable from human-written proposals
