# ProposalPilot -- Revenue Model & Business Plan

## Pricing Strategy

ProposalPilot uses **per-seat subscription pricing** with four tiers designed to capture value across the full agency size spectrum: solo consultants (Free), small agencies (Pro), mid-size agencies (Agency), and large firms (Enterprise). Each tier unlocks capabilities that directly correlate with organizational complexity and willingness to pay.

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Free Tier -- $0/month

**Target:** Solo consultants, freelancers evaluating the product, agencies in trial mode.

| Feature | Limit |
| ------- | ----- |
| Proposals per month | 3 |
| AI-generated drafts | 3 per month |
| Templates | 5 starter templates |
| Proposal analytics | Basic (open tracking only) |
| PDF export | Yes |
| E-signature | No |
| Content library | 10 blocks |
| Team members | 1 (single user) |
| CRM integration | No |
| Branding | ProposalPilot watermark on proposals |
| Support | Community (forum + docs) |

**Strategic Purpose:** The Free tier is a product-led growth engine, not a charity tier. It gives users enough value to experience the core loop (generate, edit, send, track) while creating natural upgrade triggers: hitting the 3-proposal limit, wanting e-signatures, needing team collaboration, or wanting to remove the watermark.

## Unit Economics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

| Channel | CAC | % of Acquisition |
| ------- | --- | ---------------- |
| Organic / SEO | $50 | 30% |
| LinkedIn Ads | $180 | 25% |
| CRM Marketplace (HubSpot/Salesforce) | $80 | 20% |
| Referral / Word of mouth | $30 | 15% |
| Agency conferences | $250 | 5% |
| Outbound sales (Enterprise) | $400 | 5% |
| **Blended CAC** | **$150** | **100%** |

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

```
Average Revenue per Seat per Month:  $47 (blended across tiers)
Average Seats per Organization:      4.2
Average Revenue per Org per Month:   $197
Gross Margin:                        82%
Monthly Churn Rate:                  3.5% (seats)
Average Customer Lifetime:           28.6 months (1 / 0.035)

LTV per Seat:    $47 x 28.6 x 0.82  =  $1,102
LTV per Org:     $197 x 28.6 x 0.82 =  $4,621

Blended LTV (per acquired customer): $2,124
```

### LTV:CAC Ratio

```
LTV:CAC = $2,124 / $150 = 14.2x
```

**Benchmark:** Healthy B2B SaaS targets 3x or higher. A 14.2x ratio indicates strong unit economics with room to increase acquisition spending.

### CAC Payback Period

```
Monthly Revenue per Customer:  $197
Gross Margin per Customer:     $162 ($197 x 0.82)
CAC:                           $150

Payback Period: $150 / $162 = 0.93 months (< 1 month)
```

**Benchmark:** Best-in-class B2B SaaS achieves payback under 12 months. Sub-1-month payback is exceptional and driven by the product-led growth motion (most customers self-serve on the Free tier before converting).

## Churn Analysis & Retention

### Expected Churn Rates

| Tier | Monthly Seat Churn | Annual Seat Churn | Primary Churn Reason |
| ---- | ------------------ | ----------------- | -------------------- |
| Free | N/A (no revenue) | N/A | -- |
| Pro | 5.0% | 46% | Solo consultants with irregular proposal needs |
| Agency | 3.0% | 31% | Budget cuts, competitive switch, agency closure |
| Enterprise | 1.5% | 17% | Vendor consolidation, contract non-renewal |
| **Blended** | **3.5%** | **35%** | -- |

### Churn Mitigation Strategies

| Strategy | Mechanism | Impact |
| -------- | --------- | ------ |
| **Win rate tracking** | Users see measurable improvement in win rates, creating hard ROI evidence for renewal | Reduces churn by 15-20% |
| **Content library lock-in** | As teams build reusable content blocks, switching cost increases | Reduces churn by 10-15% |
| **CRM integration depth** | Bi-directional sync makes ProposalPilot part of the deal workflow, not a standalone tool | Reduces churn by 20-25% |
| **Proposal history** | Historical proposal data and analytics are irreplaceable institutional knowledge | Reduces churn by 10% |
| **Annual contracts** | 17% discount for annual prepay locks in 12-month commitment | Eliminates monthly churn for ~40% of revenue |
| **Usage-based nudges** | Automated emails when usage drops ("You haven't created a proposal in 14 days") | Recovers 5-8% of at-risk accounts |
| **Quarterly business reviews** | Enterprise: scheduled reviews showing ROI, usage trends, and optimization opportunities | Reduces Enterprise churn by 30% |

### Net Revenue Retention (NRR)

```
NRR = (Starting MRR + Expansion - Contraction - Churned) / Starting MRR

Year 1 Target: 115%
Year 2 Target: 125%

Expansion sources:
  - Seat additions:     +8% annually per cohort
  - Tier upgrades:      +5% annually per cohort
  - Add-ons:            +2% annually per cohort

Contraction sources:
  - Seat removals:      -3% annually per cohort
  - Tier downgrades:    -2% annually per cohort

Churn:                  -35% annually (blended seat churn)

NRR Year 1: 100% + 15% expansion - 5% contraction - 35% churn = ~75% gross
Adjusted for org-level: 100% + 15% - 5% - 12% org churn = 115% NRR
```

**Note:** Seat-level churn (35%) overstates actual revenue loss because many "churned seats" are removals within organizations that remain customers. Organization-level churn is closer to 12% annually, and expansion within retained organizations more than compensates.

## Expansion Revenue Strategy

### Seat Expansion

- **Trigger:** When a team member needs to create or edit proposals, they need a seat
- **Mechanism:** Self-serve seat addition in billing settings, prorated for current billing period
- **Average expansion:** Organizations grow from 3.2 seats at signup to 5.8 seats by Month 12
- **Revenue impact:** +35% revenue per cohort over first 12 months

### Tier Upgrades

- **Free to Pro:** Triggered by 3-proposal limit, watermark removal desire, e-signature need
- **Pro to Agency:** Triggered by second team member, CRM integration need, win rate analytics
- **Agency to Enterprise:** Triggered by SSO requirement, white-label need, SLA requirement
- **Average upgrade timeline:** Free to Pro (14 days), Pro to Agency (4 months), Agency to Enterprise (8 months)

### Premium Template Marketplace (Year 2)

- **Model:** Curated templates designed by professional proposal writers and designers
- **Pricing:** $9-$29 per template (one-time purchase)
- **Revenue split:** 70% to template creator, 30% to ProposalPilot
- **Volume target:** 500 templates available by end of Year 2, $15K/month marketplace revenue
- **Curation:** All templates reviewed for quality, branded with performance data (win rate of proposals using this template)

### Analytics API (Year 2)

- **Model:** $49/month add-on for Agency tier, included in Enterprise
- **Use case:** Agencies that want to build custom dashboards, integrate proposal analytics into their BI tools, or share win rate data with their own clients
- **Target:** 10% of Agency tier subscribers ($49 x 500 = $24,500/month)

### Consulting Services (Year 2+)

- **Model:** Proposal strategy consulting powered by platform data
- **Services:** Win rate audits, pricing strategy workshops, proposal template design, onboarding and training
- **Pricing:** $500/session (1 hour), $2,500/audit (comprehensive), $5,000/workshop (half-day)
- **Target:** 20 sessions/month = $10,000-$20,000/month
- **Strategic value:** Drives Enterprise tier adoption, provides qualitative user insights, builds case studies
