## 💡 Key Factors in Difficulty Ranking

1. **AI Complexity** - Simple text generation vs. computer vision vs. specialized ML models
2. **Real-time Requirements** - Batch processing vs. live video analysis
3. **Domain Expertise** - General business logic vs. medical/legal/electrical engineering
4. **Integration Complexity** - Single API vs. multiple enterprise systems
5. **Platform Challenges** - Web app vs. native mobile vs. desktop system integration
6. **Performance Requirements** - Simple CRUD vs. video processing vs. 3D rendering
7. **Regulatory Compliance** - Standard app vs. HIPAA/financial/legal requirements
8. **Architecture Complexity** - Simple client-server vs. distributed systems
