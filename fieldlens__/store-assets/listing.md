### Description
Get real-time AI coaching for any trade task — just point your camera. Fieldlens provides instant visual feedback for electricians, plumbers, HVAC techs, and carpenters through AI-powered camera coaching.

Built with licensed master tradespeople, Fieldlens is the AI trade coach that lives in your pocket and shows up on every job. Whether you are an apprentice learning the trade or a journeyman tackling an unfamiliar system, Fieldlens gives you instant, accurate guidance from the world's most experienced virtual trade coach — without waiting for your foreman to be available.

**What Fieldlens Does for You:**

- Point your camera at any task and receive real-time AI visual coaching in under 3 seconds
- Step-by-step task guides walk you through every trade procedure from rough-in to finish
- GPS status chip shows your job location logged automatically for each coaching session
- Voice note FAB (floating action button) captures hands-free field notes while working
- Weather widget keeps current site conditions visible without leaving the app
- Schedule time blocks organize your day's tasks in a clear visual timeline
- AI catch log tracks every error identified and code violation avoided per job

**Key Features:**

**AI Camera Coaching**
Point your camera at your work — a pipe fitting, a panel wiring run, a duct connection, a stud framing layout — and Fieldlens AI analyzes what it sees in real time. Within 3 seconds you receive specific, actionable coaching: what looks correct, what needs adjustment, what the applicable code requires, and what the next step is. The camera coaching overlay highlights the exact area of concern so there is no ambiguity.

**Step-by-Step Task Guides**
Browse task guides organized by trade and procedure — plumbing rough-in, electrical service panel replacement, HVAC refrigerant line set, framing, tile work, and hundreds more. Follow each step with photo references showing what correct completion looks like. Guides are written by licensed master tradespeople and reviewed for code accuracy.

**Trade-Specific Coaching**
Fieldlens is not a generic AI assistant — it understands the specifics of each trade. Ask about a P-trap installation and it responds with plumbing code references. Ask about a GFCI circuit and it responds with NEC requirements. The AI coaching is calibrated to journeyman-level technical precision.

**GPS Job Logging**
The GPS status chip on your dashboard automatically logs your location for each coaching session. Your complete job history — location, trade, task, AI findings — is organized chronologically. Useful for apprenticeship hour tracking, liability documentation, and performance review.

**Voice Note FAB**
The floating action button for voice notes stays accessible on every screen. Tap once to start recording a field observation hands-free. Notes are transcribed automatically and attached to the relevant job record.

**Weather Widget**
Current weather conditions for your job location appear directly on the dashboard — no switching apps. Rain, wind, and temperature affect trade work. Fieldlens keeps the weather visible so you can plan accordingly.

**Schedule Time Blocks**
Your day's tasks organized in a visual schedule timeline. Each time block shows the trade, task name, estimated duration, and AI coaching session count. Stay on schedule and track how your time is actually spent.

**AI Coaching History**
Every AI coaching session is saved with the original camera input, AI analysis, code reference, and your task outcome. Review your history to track skill development, identify recurring issues, and build a portfolio of completed work.

**Apprenticeship Hour Tracking**
Log coaching sessions by trade and task type to generate apprenticeship hour reports for your union or licensing board. Fieldlens makes hour documentation automatic and accurate.

**Who Uses Fieldlens:**
- Apprentices in plumbing, electrical, HVAC, and carpentry — learning correct technique in real time
- Journeymen tackling unfamiliar systems or complex code requirements on new job types
- Independent contractors verifying their work meets current code before the inspection
- Trade instructors using AI coaching as a teaching tool in the classroom and field
- Foremen who want apprentices to have AI support between direct supervision moments

Fieldlens fills the gap between classroom knowledge and real-world execution — with AI coaching that is available at 6am on a Saturday when the foreman is not. Every error caught before the inspection is a callback avoided. Every code question answered in the moment is an hour of rework prevented.
