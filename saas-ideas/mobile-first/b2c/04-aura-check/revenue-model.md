## Churn Analysis

### Monthly Churn Target: 4%

| Churn Factor | Mitigation |
|--------------|-----------|
| **"My skin is fine, why pay?"** | Health correlation insights provide ongoing value even when skin is clear. Streak gamification. |
| **Perceived inaccuracy** | Continuous prompt improvement. Dermatologist validation loop. Transparency about AI limitations. |
| **Cost sensitivity** | Annual plan at 29% discount ($9.17/mo effective). Price anchored against $221/visit. |
| **Feature fatigue** | Monthly feature updates. Seasonal content (sun safety in summer, dryness in winter). |
| **Competitor** | No direct competitor offers comprehensive skin + health monitoring. Switching cost is high (historical data). |

### Retention Drivers

| Driver | Mechanism |
|--------|-----------|
| **Longitudinal data lock-in** | 6+ months of skin history is irreplaceable. Users cannot recreate this data elsewhere. |
| **Daily habit** | Push notification at same time daily. Streak counter with celebrations. |
| **Health correlation depth** | Insights improve with more data. Users see "3 more weeks of data needed for diet correlation." |
| **Seasonal value** | Sun damage tracking in summer, dryness in winter, acne through all seasons. |
| **Peace of mind** | For mole monitors, the value is "knowing nothing has changed." This renews monthly. |

## Lifetime Value (LTV)

### LTV Calculation

| Component | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **ARPU** | $16.00/month |
| **Average Lifetime** | 30 months |
| **Gross Margin** | 93% |
| **LTV (gross)** | $16.00 * 30 = **$480** |
| **LTV (net of COGS)** | $480 * 0.93 = **$446** |
| **LTV (net of platform fees)** | $446 * 0.85 (15% App Store) = **$379** |

### LTV:CAC Ratio

| Metric | Value | Benchmark |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| **LTV** | $480 (gross) | |
| **CAC** | $20 | |
| **LTV:CAC** | **24:1** | Benchmark: 3:1 minimum for healthy SaaS |
| **CAC Payback Period** | 1.25 months | $20 CAC / $16 ARPU = 1.25 months |

This LTV:CAC ratio is exceptionally strong for three reasons:
1. Health apps have the longest retention of any app category (30 months average)
2. AI inference costs are negligible relative to subscription price ($0.91/month vs. $16/month ARPU)
3. Consumer willingness to pay for health monitoring is the highest of any app category

## Pricing Psychology

### Why $12.99 and $24.99

| Factor | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Anchor: Dermatologist visit** | $221 per visit. $12.99/month for continuous monitoring is 94% cheaper than a single visit. |
| **Anchor: Dermatologist wait time** | 35-day average wait. Instant AI analysis reframes the value proposition from cost to access. |
| **Comparable: Health apps** | Noom ($59/mo), MyFitnessPal Premium ($19.99/mo), Headspace ($12.99/mo). Aura Check's $12.99 is at the low end of health app pricing. |
| **Charm pricing** | $12.99 feels substantially less than $13.00. $24.99 feels like "mid-twenties" not "twenty-five." |
| **Annual discount** | 29% discount for annual plans ($109.99/year = $9.17/month). Rewards commitment, reduces churn, improves cash flow. |
| **Premium+ justification** | The $24.99 tier includes a telehealth credit worth $75-150. Users perceive they are getting the monitoring for "free" on top of the telehealth value. |

### Price Elasticity Testing Plan

| Phase | Test |
|-------|------|
| **Beta** | Free only. Validate engagement and willingness to use daily. |
| **Launch** | $12.99/$24.99 as baseline. |
| **Month 3** | A/B test $9.99 vs. $12.99 vs. $14.99 for Premium. Measure conversion rate and LTV. |
| **Month 6** | A/B test $19.99 vs. $24.99 vs. $29.99 for Premium+. Measure Premium-to-Plus upgrade rate. |
| **Month 9** | Introduce lifetime plan option ($299 one-time) for early adopters. |
