| Jobs per month | 500 |

| AI Job Intake | Basic (text parsing only) |
| Real-Time Re-Routing | Not included |
| Analytics | Basic dashboard (today's metrics only) |

| Onboarding | Self-serve (video tutorials) |

**Why $99:** Low enough to be an easy "yes" for a small company owner ($3.30/day). Higher than free-tier alternatives but justified by immediate, measurable time savings. Positioned below Jobber's Pro plan ($169) and far below ServiceTitan's entry point ($2,000+).

## Why 40% Trial-to-Paid Conversion

RouteAI's expected trial-to-paid conversion rate of 40% is above the B2B SaaS average of 15-25%. This is justified by three factors:

**1. Measurable ROI Within the Trial Period**

Unlike CRM or marketing tools where ROI is fuzzy, RouteAI produces a concrete number on day one: "Your optimized routes save 3.2 hours of drive time across your fleet today." The dispatcher can see the before/after comparison immediately. By the end of a 14-day trial, the company has quantified the savings and can calculate the ROI in dollar terms.

Example: A 15-technician plumbing company trials RouteAI.
- Day 1: AI optimizes routes, shows 28% less drive time
- Day 7: Dispatcher reports "we completed 8 more jobs this week than usual"
- Day 14: Owner calculates: 8 extra jobs/week x $200 avg ticket = $1,600/week in additional revenue
- $1,600/week vs. $249/month subscription? That is a 6.4x ROI. The conversion is almost automatic.

**2. Operational Dependency Forms Quickly**

Within a week, the dispatcher is using RouteAI's schedule board instead of their whiteboard. Technicians are used to the mobile app and turn-by-turn navigation. Customers are receiving SMS notifications. Going back to the old way feels like a downgrade. The switching cost is not contractual — it is operational.

**3. ICP Targeting Reduces Unqualified Trials**

RouteAI targets companies with 5-50 technicians running 20-100 jobs/day. These companies have the exact pain that RouteAI solves. By qualifying prospects before they trial (LinkedIn outreach targets specific company sizes, trade publication ads reach the right audience), the trial pool is pre-qualified.

### Churn Reduction Strategies

**1. Monthly ROI Email:**
"Hi Mike, RouteAI saved ABC Plumbing 142 hours of drive time this month, equivalent to $10,650 in recovered technician productivity. Your subscription cost: $249."

This email goes out automatically on the first of every month to every company owner. It makes the ROI undeniable and makes churn conversations difficult for anyone proposing to cancel.

**2. Feature Expansion (Negative Churn):**
As companies grow and use more features (CRM, job costing, inventory), they upgrade to higher tiers. The goal is net negative revenue churn — expansion revenue from existing customers exceeds lost revenue from churned customers.

**3. Annual Pricing Discount:**
Offer 20% discount for annual prepayment. This locks in revenue and reduces churn (customers are less likely to cancel a pre-paid annual plan).

## Sales Channels

### Channel 1: Direct LinkedIn Outreach

**Why:** HVAC/plumbing company owners and operations managers are active on LinkedIn. They respond to personalized messages that reference their business specifically.

**Process:**
1. Identify prospects via LinkedIn Sales Navigator (filters: industry = HVAC/Plumbing/Electrical, company size = 5-50, title = Owner/President/Operations Manager)
2. Send connection request with personalized note referencing their company
3. Follow up with value message: "Companies like yours typically waste 15-25% of technician capacity on driving between jobs. Our AI cuts that by 30%. Interested in seeing how this would work for [Company Name]?"
4. Book 15-minute demo call
5. Run live optimization demo with their service area
6. Start 14-day free trial
7. Follow up at day 3, 7, 14 with usage data and ROI calculations

**Economics:**
- Connection rate: 40%
- Response rate: 15%
- Demo rate: 30% of responses
- Trial rate: 60% of demos
- Close rate: 40% of trials
- 500 outreaches → 200 connections → 30 responses → 9 demos → 5 trials → 2 paid
- CAC: 500 outreaches x $0.50 (Sales Navigator + time) + $200 (sales time) = $450

### Channel 2: Trade Publication Advertising

**Why:** Home service professionals read industry publications for technology recommendations. An ad in ACHR News reaches 50,000+ HVAC decision makers.

**Key Publications:**

| Publication | Audience | Ad Cost (monthly) | Expected Leads |
|------------|----------|-------------------|----------------|
| ACHR News | 50K HVAC professionals | $2,000-4,000 | 20-40 leads |
| Contracting Business | 40K HVAC/plumbing | $1,500-3,000 | 15-30 leads |

| Pest Management Prof. | 20K pest control | $800-1,500 | 5-15 leads |
| Cleaning & Maint. Mgmt | 25K cleaning companies | $1,000-2,000 | 8-15 leads |

**Ad Creative:** Focus on the concrete ROI number. "AI routes that save your technicians 2 hours of driving per day. Start free trial."

### Channel 3: Equipment Distributor Partnerships

**Why:** Equipment distributors have direct relationships with every HVAC/plumbing company in their territory. A recommendation from a trusted distributor is the strongest possible referral.

**Partnership Model:**
- Distributor refers their contractor customers to RouteAI
- RouteAI provides co-branded onboarding and support
- Distributor earns $100-200 per converted customer + 10% revenue share for 12 months
- RouteAI gets access to distributor's customer base (1,000+ contractors per territory)

**Target Distributors:**
| Distributor | Locations | Contractors Served |
|------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Carrier Enterprise | 1,000+ | 50,000+ |
| Watsco | 670+ | 40,000+ |
| Lennox distributors | 200+ | 25,000+ |
| Trane Supply | 300+ | 30,000+ |
| Ferguson | 1,700+ | 100,000+ (plumbing + HVAC) |

Even converting 1% of one major distributor's contractor base would yield 400-500 companies.

### Channel 4: Referral Program

**Structure:**
- Existing customer refers another home service company
- Referrer receives $100 account credit per signed customer
- Referred company receives 30-day extended trial (instead of 14)
- Both parties get "Referral Champion" badge (gamification)

**Why $100:** Low enough to be sustainable (33% of first month's ARPU). High enough to motivate active referrals. Home service company owners talk to each other — at trade events, distributor meetings, and local business groups.

**Expected Referral Rate:** 15% of customers refer at least one other company within 12 months. Average referrals per referring customer: 2.3.

**Referral CAC:** $100 credit + $0 acquisition cost = $100 (lowest CAC channel by far).

## Revenue Expansion Strategy

### Expansion Revenue (Negative Net Churn)

The goal is for expansion revenue from existing customers to exceed churned revenue, resulting in net negative revenue churn.

**Expansion Triggers:**

| Trigger | Expansion | Revenue Impact |
|---------|-----------|----------------|
| Hire more technicians (cross Starter → Growth threshold) | Tier upgrade | +$150/month |
| Hire more technicians (cross Growth → Scale threshold) | Tier upgrade | +$250/month |

| SMS overage | Usage-based billing | +$20-50/month |
| API integrations requested | Scale plan required | +$250/month |
| Equipment financing integration | Referral revenue | +$50-200/month |

**Target:** 5% monthly expansion revenue rate (expansion revenue / starting MRR).

### Long-Term Revenue Diversification

**Phase 1 (Months 1-18): Core SaaS Revenue**
- 100% of revenue from subscription tiers
- $99-499/company/month

**Phase 2 (Months 18-36): Value-Added Services**
- Equipment financing referral fees (1-3% of financed amount)
- Premium SMS packages for high-volume companies
- Data analytics and reporting add-ons
- API access for custom integrations
- Target: 15% of revenue from add-ons

**Phase 3 (Months 36+): Platform Revenue**
- Marketplace for service parts ordering (take rate on transactions)
- Insurance partnership revenue share
- Delivery/logistics expansion
- International markets
- Target: 30% of revenue from non-subscription sources
