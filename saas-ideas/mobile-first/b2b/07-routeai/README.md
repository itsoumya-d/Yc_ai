# RouteAI

**AI dispatch that saves 2 hours per tech per day.**

RouteAI is an AI-powered dispatch and route optimization platform built for last-mile home service businesses. HVAC companies, plumbers, pest control operators, and cleaning services use RouteAI to eliminate wasted drive time, auto-schedule jobs intelligently, and deliver tighter appointment windows to customers. The result: 30% less drive time, more jobs per technician per day, and dramatically better customer satisfaction.

---

## The Pitch

Every day, 600,000+ home service companies in the US send technicians out on routes planned by gut instinct, sticky notes, or basic calendar tools. The result is chaos: technicians crisscross cities, customers get 4-hour appointment windows, and companies lose 15-25% of productive capacity to inefficient routing alone.

RouteAI fixes this with AI that actually understands service operations. Not generic fleet tracking. Not enterprise software designed for 500-truck operations. RouteAI is purpose-built for the 5-50 technician company that is too big for manual dispatch but too small for ServiceTitan.

**How it works:**

1. **AI Job Intake** — Customer calls and texts are processed by AI into structured, schedulable jobs with the right skill requirements, time estimates, and priority levels.
2. **Constraint-Based Scheduling** — AI considers technician skills, certifications, location, customer time preferences, traffic patterns, and job dependencies to build optimal daily schedules.
3. **Route Optimization** — Google OR-Tools solves the vehicle routing problem across the entire fleet, minimizing total drive time while respecting all constraints.
4. **Real-Time Re-Routing** — When a job runs long, gets cancelled, or an emergency comes in, the entire fleet re-optimizes in seconds. Not just one technician, the entire fleet.
5. **Customer Notifications** — Automated SMS with tight appointment windows (30 min instead of 4 hours), real-time ETA updates, and completion confirmations.

Two native mobile apps: one for dispatchers to manage the operation, one for technicians to navigate their routes and complete jobs. Real-time sync between both.

## The Market Problem

### Quantified Waste

According to McKinsey, home service companies lose **15-25% of productive capacity** to inefficient routing and scheduling. For a company with 20 technicians each billing $75/hour, that translates to:

- **$450K-750K per year** in lost productive capacity
- **2+ hours per technician per day** spent driving instead of working
- **4-hour appointment windows** that frustrate customers and cause no-shows
- **15-20% of jobs** requiring rescheduling due to timing conflicts

The average home service technician spends 35% of their working day driving between jobs. RouteAI cuts that to 22%, reclaiming 1.5-2 hours of billable time per technician per day.

### Why Now

1. **AI capability inflection** — LLMs can now parse unstructured customer requests into structured jobs. Constraint-based reasoning has reached the level needed for real-time schedule optimization.
2. **Google Maps Platform maturity** — The Distance Matrix and Directions APIs are now fast and affordable enough for continuous fleet re-optimization.
3. **Mobile-first workforce** — Every technician carries a smartphone. The app delivery model is natural for this workforce.
4. **Post-COVID demand surge** — Home services demand is at all-time highs. Companies are desperate to squeeze more capacity from existing teams rather than hiring.

### Why We Win

1. **ServiceTitan** ($9.5B) is enterprise-focused. Minimum contract is $2,000+/month with 3-6 month implementation. The 20-technician plumbing company cannot afford it and does not need it. RouteAI costs 90% less and deploys in a day.

2. **Jobber** ($155M raised) is a great CRM and invoicing tool but has zero AI optimization. Routes are planned manually. Scheduling is drag-and-drop on a calendar. There is no intelligence. RouteAI integrates with Jobber or replaces it entirely.

3. **Route4Me** is a generic route optimization tool built for delivery fleets. It does not understand service business constraints: technician skills (EPA certified for refrigerant?), job dependencies (inspection before repair), customer preferences (only available 2-4 PM), or real-time service duration uncertainty. RouteAI is built ground-up for these constraints.

4. **Housecall Pro** ($125M raised) focuses on marketing and customer acquisition. It helps companies get customers but does nothing to optimize how those customers are served. RouteAI makes each customer visit more efficient.

### Defensibility

- **Network effects in route data:** More jobs processed means better time estimates, better traffic modeling, better optimization. A company using RouteAI for 6 months has meaningfully better route predictions than day one.
- **Switching costs:** Once dispatch workflows are built around RouteAI, switching is operationally painful. Technicians are trained, customers expect SMS updates, dispatchers depend on the AI suggestions.
- **Vertical depth:** Generic route tools cannot match the depth of understanding service operations. Every feature is designed for the reality of field service: jobs run long, emergencies appear, technicians call in sick, parts are out of stock.
- **Data moat:** Historical job completion data across thousands of companies creates industry-specific time estimates that no competitor can replicate without the same volume.

## Quick Start

```bash
# Clone the repository
git clone https://github.com/yourusername/routeai.git
cd routeai

# Install dependencies
npm install

# Set up environment variables
cp .env.example .env
# Add your API keys: Supabase, Google Maps, OpenAI, Twilio, Stripe

# Start the dispatcher app
cd apps/dispatcher
npx expo start

# Start the technician app (separate terminal)
cd apps/technician
npx expo start
```
