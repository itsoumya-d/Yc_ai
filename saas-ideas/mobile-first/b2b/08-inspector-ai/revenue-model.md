# Revenue Model — Inspector AI

> Pricing strategy, unit economics, acquisition channels, and the path to $1M MRR.

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Tier 1: Free Trial

**Price**: $0 for 14 days, up to 5 inspections

**Included:**
- Full AI damage detection (5 inspections)
- Automated report generation (5 reports)
- PDF export
- Basic property scoring
- Single user
- Email support

**Purpose**: Let adjusters experience the product with real inspections before committing. Five inspections is enough for 2-3 days of work — enough to see the value, not enough to replace a paid tool.

**Conversion target**: 25% trial-to-paid within 14 days.

---

### Tier 2: Pro

**Price**: $79/month per adjuster (billed monthly) or $69/month per adjuster (billed annually = $828/year)

**Included:**
- Unlimited AI-powered inspections
- Unlimited automated reports
- All damage assessment features
- Offline mode with full sync
- PDF, DOCX, and XML export
- 10 carrier report templates
- Property history tracking
- 50GB photo storage per adjuster
- Voice-to-text notes
- Priority email + chat support
- Basic analytics

**Target customer**: Independent adjusters and small adjusting firms (1-10 adjusters) who handle 10-30 inspections per month.

**Value proposition math**: An adjuster spending 3 hours per inspection on reports (at $50/hour effective rate) saves ~2 hours with Inspector AI = $100/inspection saved. At 15 inspections/month, that is $1,500/month in time savings for a $79/month cost. **19:1 ROI**.

---

### Tier 3: Enterprise

**Price**: $49/month per adjuster (25+ seats minimum, billed annually = $588/adjuster/year)

**Minimum contract**: 25 seats = $1,225/month ($14,700/year)

**Included:**
- Everything in Pro
- Unlimited carrier report templates (all 50+)
- Team management and assignment routing
- Advanced analytics and reporting dashboard
- Custom report branding (logo, colors, letterhead)
- Quality review workflow (manager approval)
- SSO (SAML/OIDC) integration
- Custom API integrations
- Dedicated account manager
- Phone + priority support
- SLA: 99.9% uptime guarantee
- Data export and compliance tools
- Custom training sessions
- 200GB photo storage per adjuster

**Target customer**: Mid-to-large adjusting firms (25-500 adjusters), managing general agents (MGAs), and carrier in-house inspection teams.

**Volume discounts:**

| Seats | Price per Adjuster/Month |
|---|---|
| 25-49 | $49 |
| 50-99 | $44 |
| 100-249 | $39 |
| 250-499 | $34 |
| 500+ | Custom pricing |

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

```

  ARPU:               $79/month
  Gross margin:        82% (after API costs ~$14/user/month)
  Monthly churn:       4% (blended)
  Avg lifetime:        25 months (1/churn)
  LTV:                 $79 x 0.82 x 25 = $1,620

Enterprise LTV (per seat):
  ARPU:               $49/month
  Gross margin:        85% (lower API cost per user at scale)
  Monthly churn:       1.5% (enterprise contracts)
  Avg lifetime:        67 months (1/churn)
  LTV:                 $49 x 0.85 x 67 = $2,790

Enterprise LTV (per organization, avg 50 seats):
  LTV per org:         $2,790 x 50 = $139,500
```

```
Pro:
  Monthly contribution margin: $79 x 0.82 = $64.78
```

**Both segments pay back CAC within the first billing cycle.** This is exceptionally strong and supports aggressive investment in growth.

## Churn Analysis

### Why Customers Churn (Predicted)

| Reason | % of Churn | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| AI accuracy not meeting expectations | 30% | Continuous model improvement, adjuster feedback loop |
| Too expensive relative to inspection volume | 20% | Usage-based pricing tier or seasonal plans |
| Switched to carrier-provided tool | 15% | Deepen carrier integrations to become the preferred tool |
| Adjuster retired or left the industry | 15% | Normal attrition — not addressable |
| Poor customer support experience | 10% | Dedicated support team, in-app chat, response SLA |
| Competitor offering | 10% | Feature velocity, switching cost via data lock-in |

### Churn Reduction Strategies

1. **Data lock-in**: Every inspection, photo, and report is stored in Inspector AI. Switching means losing historical data and property history — a powerful retention mechanism.
2. **Carrier template advantage**: The more carrier templates a customer uses, the harder it is to switch to a tool that lacks those specific formats.
3. **Seasonal flexibility**: Offer "seasonal pause" for adjusters with low winter volume instead of cancellation. $10/month to keep account active with data access.
4. **Quality improvement loop**: Monthly model accuracy reports showing how the AI improved based on their feedback.
5. **Community**: Adjuster community forum and knowledge base creates social switching costs.

### Churn Targets

|---|---|---|---|---|
| Pro (monthly) | 6% | 5% | 4% | 3% |
| Pro (annual) | 1.5% | 1.2% | 1.0% | 0.8% |
| Enterprise | 1.0% | 0.8% | 0.6% | 0.5% |

## Expansion Revenue

Expansion revenue is critical for reaching $1M MRR. Here are the levers:

### E1: Per-Inspection Overage Fees

For customers who exceed their plan's included inspections (if usage caps are introduced on lower tiers).

**Pricing**: $3 per additional inspection beyond plan limit.
**Expected adoption**: 15% of Pro users hit the cap 3+ months per year.

### E2: API Access

Third-party developers and carriers can access Inspector AI's damage detection API directly.

**Pricing:**
- Developer tier: $0.50 per API call (damage assessment)
- Enterprise API: $0.25 per call (volume discounted)
- Minimum commitment: $500/month

**Target customers**: Carrier claims systems, proptech platforms, restoration companies.
**Expected revenue contribution**: 5-10% of total revenue by Year 3.

### E3: White-Label Program

Adjusting firms and carriers can white-label Inspector AI under their own brand.

**Pricing:**
- Setup fee: $25,000 one-time
- Ongoing: $35/seat/month (minimum 100 seats = $3,500/month)
- Custom integration: $10,000-50,000 (project-based)

**Target customers**: Top 20 adjusting firms, regional carriers.
**Expected revenue contribution**: 10-15% of total revenue by Year 3.

### E4: Premium Add-Ons

| Add-On | Price | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Drone integration module | $29/month per adjuster | DJI integration for aerial inspections |
| AR damage overlay | $19/month per adjuster | Augmented reality measurement tools |

| Extended storage (500GB) | $19/month per adjuster | For high-volume photo documentation |

### E5: Seat Expansion within Enterprise Accounts

As firms grow or realize the value of Inspector AI, they add more seats.

**Net revenue retention target**: 120% (meaning existing customers grow revenue by 20% year-over-year through seat expansion and add-ons).
