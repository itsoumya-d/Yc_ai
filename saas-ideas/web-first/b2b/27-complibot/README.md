## The Problem

Startups face a compliance paradox: enterprise customers demand SOC 2, GDPR, and HIPAA compliance before signing contracts, but the cost and complexity of achieving compliance is prohibitive for early-stage companies.

### Pain Points

1. **Deal Blockers**: 70% of startups report losing enterprise deals because they lack SOC 2 or equivalent compliance certifications. Procurement teams at Fortune 500 companies will not sign a contract without seeing a SOC 2 Type II report.

2. **Prohibitive Cost**: Achieving SOC 2 compliance through traditional consulting costs $50,000 to $200,000 per engagement, plus $150,000 to $250,000 annually for a full-time compliance hire. For a Series A startup with $5M in funding, that is 4-10% of total capital.

3. **Time Drain**: The traditional compliance journey takes 6 to 12 months. During that period, founders and engineering leads spend 15-25 hours per week on compliance tasks instead of building product.

4. **Knowledge Gap**: Most startup teams have zero compliance experience. They do not know what a Trust Services Criteria is, how to write an access control policy, or what evidence an auditor actually needs.

5. **Ongoing Burden**: Compliance is not a one-time event. SOC 2 Type II requires continuous monitoring and evidence collection over a 3 to 12 month observation period. GDPR requires ongoing data mapping and breach response readiness.

6. **Multi-Framework Complexity**: As startups grow, they need multiple frameworks simultaneously. A health-tech SaaS company selling to enterprises needs SOC 2 AND HIPAA AND possibly GDPR. Each framework has overlapping but distinct requirements.

## The Solution

CompliBot replaces the $200K compliance team with an AI-powered platform that automates 80% of the compliance journey:

### How It Works

1. **Connect** -- Link your AWS, GCP, GitHub, and identity provider accounts. CompliBot scans your infrastructure in minutes, not months.

2. **Assess** -- The AI maps your current state against your target framework(s). You get a compliance score, a categorized gap analysis, and a prioritized remediation plan.

3. **Generate** -- CompliBot generates all required policies (information security, access control, incident response, data classification, etc.) customized to your actual technology stack and company structure.

4. **Remediate** -- Follow the task board to close gaps. CompliBot provides specific, actionable remediation steps, not generic checklists. Tasks are assigned to team members with due dates.

5. **Collect** -- Evidence collection is automated. CompliBot pulls configuration screenshots, access logs, deployment records, and change management evidence continuously.

6. **Monitor** -- Continuous monitoring alerts you when configurations drift out of compliance. No more "we passed the audit but broke compliance the next week."

7. **Audit** -- When your auditor arrives, the Audit Room provides a secure portal with all evidence organized by control. Auditors love it, which speeds up the audit and reduces billable hours.
