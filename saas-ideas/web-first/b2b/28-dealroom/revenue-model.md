# DealRoom -- Revenue Model

## Pricing Strategy

DealRoom uses a **per-seat, per-month SaaS pricing model** with three tiers designed to capture value from individual reps (Starter), full sales teams (Professional), and enterprise organizations (Enterprise). Pricing is anchored against competitors (Gong at $100+/seat, Clari at custom pricing) while offering a lower entry point to reduce adoption friction.

### Pricing Philosophy

- **Value-based, not cost-based.** Pricing reflects the revenue impact DealRoom delivers (more deals closed, faster cycles), not the cost of AI API calls.
- **Land with reps, expand to teams.** Starter plan gets individual reps in the door. Professional unlocks team-wide value. Enterprise captures large organizations.
- **No free tier.** B2B sales tools with free tiers attract tire-kickers, not serious buyers. A 14-day free trial with full Professional features replaces a free tier.
- **Annual discount.** 20% discount on annual billing to improve cash flow and reduce churn.

## Unit Economics

### Core Metrics

| Metric | Value | Benchmark (B2B SaaS) |
|--------|-------|-----------------------|
| **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)** | $99/seat/month (blended) | $50-150 |
| **Average Seats Per Team** | 10 (growing to 15 at scale) | 5-20 |
| **Average Contract Value (ACV)** | $11,880/year (10 seats x $99 x 12) | $10K-50K |
| **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** | $400 per seat | $200-600 |
| **Customer Lifetime** | 5 years (60 months) | 3-7 years |
| **Lifetime Value (LTV)** | $5,940 per seat ($99 x 60) | $3K-10K |
| **LTV:CAC Ratio** | 14.9x | 3-5x (good), 5x+ (excellent) |
| **CAC Payback Period** | 4 months | 6-18 months |
| **Gross Margin** | 82% | 70-85% |

### CAC Breakdown

| Channel | CAC per Seat | % of Acquisition |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Content Marketing / SEO** | $150 | 25% |
| **LinkedIn Ads (VP Sales targeting)** | $500 | 20% |
| **Salesforce AppExchange** | $200 | 20% |
| **Conference / Event** | $800 | 10% |
| **Referral / Word of Mouth** | $50 | 15% |
| **Outbound Sales** | $600 | 10% |
| **Blended Average** | **$400** | 100% |

### Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)

| Cost Category | Per Seat/Month | % of Revenue |
|---------------|---------------|-------------|
| **OpenAI (GPT-4o + Whisper)** | $2.50 | 2.5% |
| **Infrastructure (Vercel + Supabase)** | $3.00 | 3.0% |
| **Contact Enrichment (Apollo.io)** | $1.50 | 1.5% |
| **Support Cost** | $5.00 | 5.1% |
| **Payment Processing (Stripe)** | $2.87 | 2.9% |
| **Other (monitoring, email delivery)** | $3.00 | 3.0% |
| **Total COGS** | **$17.87** | **18.0%** |
| **Gross Margin** | **$81.13** | **82.0%** |

### LTV Calculation

```
LTV = ARPU x Gross Margin x Customer Lifetime
LTV = $99/mo x 82% x 60 months
LTV = $4,870 (per seat, gross margin adjusted)

Simple LTV (revenue-based) = $99 x 60 = $5,940 per seat
Team LTV = $5,940 x 10 seats = $59,400 per team
```

## Churn Analysis and Mitigation

### Churn Targets

| Metric | Year 1 Target | Year 2 Target | Mature Target |
|--------|--------------|--------------|---------------|
| **Logo Churn (Monthly)** | < 5% | < 3% | < 2% |
| **Revenue Churn (Monthly)** | < 4% | < 2% | < 1% |
| **Net Revenue Retention** | 110% | 125% | 135% |

### Churn Risk Factors

| Risk Factor | Probability | Mitigation |
|-------------|-------------|------------|
| CRM sync breaks and data goes stale | High (early) | Sync health dashboard, auto-retry, admin alerts, dedicated sync engineering |
| AI scores feel inaccurate or inconsistent | Medium | Transparent scoring factors, manual override with feedback, weekly model retraining |
| Low rep adoption (managers buy, reps ignore) | High | Daily email digest, one-click actions, mobile-friendly alerts, onboarding checklist |
| Champion leaves the customer org | Medium | Multi-thread into multiple contacts at customer org, identify backup champions |
| Budget cuts at customer company | Low-Medium | Demonstrate ROI with quarterly impact reports (deals influenced, time saved) |
| Competitor releases similar feature | Medium | Maintain 6-month feature lead, focus on unified intelligence (not single-feature tools) |

### Churn Mitigation Playbook

| Trigger | Action | Owner |
|---------|--------|-------|
| CRM sync error count > 5 in 24 hours | Auto-email admin + DealRoom CS team | System |
| User login drops > 50% week-over-week | CSM reaches out with "re-engagement" email | Customer Success |
| No AI emails sent in 14 days | In-app prompt: "You haven't used AI email generation lately" | Product |
| Trial user does not connect CRM in 48 hours | Automated email with setup guide + offer for live onboarding call | Marketing |
| Enterprise contract renewal 90 days out | CSM schedules QBR with ROI report | Customer Success |

The average DealRoom customer starts with 8 seats and expands to 15 seats over 18 months. This expansion is driven by:

1. **Proof of value:** Initial team sees results, manager adds remaining reps
2. **Adjacent team adoption:** SDR team, Customer Success team, Solutions Engineering
3. **Organic hiring:** Growing sales teams add new hires to DealRoom during onboarding
4. **Company-wide mandate:** VP Sales mandates DealRoom for all reps after seeing forecast accuracy improvement

### DealRoom Pricing Advantages

1. **Transparent pricing.** Published pricing on the website, no "contact sales for a quote" barrier.
2. **Lower entry point.** $49/seat vs. $100+/seat for competitors. Starter plan lets teams validate before committing.
3. **All-in-one value.** Competitors charge separately for calls (Gong), forecasting (Clari), and activity capture (People.ai). DealRoom bundles everything.
4. **Self-serve purchase.** Starter and Professional plans can be purchased with a credit card. No 3-month enterprise sales cycle for a 10-person team.
