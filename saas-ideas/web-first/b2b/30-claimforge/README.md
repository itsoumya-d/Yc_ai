## The Problem

False Claims Act cases are among the most impactful tools for fighting government fraud. In 2024 alone, $2.2 billion was recovered through FCA actions. Yet the process of building these cases is painfully manual:

1. **Document Overload**: Whistleblower attorneys receive thousands of invoices, contracts, emails, and financial records. Reviewing them manually takes months.
2. **Pattern Blindness**: Fraud patterns (duplicate billing, phantom vendors, quality substitution) hide in plain sight across thousands of line items. Human reviewers miss 40-60% of anomalies.
3. **Evidence Organization**: Building a chronological, citation-ready evidence package requires painstaking manual assembly. Attorneys spend 60% of their time organizing, not analyzing.
4. **Statistical Proof**: Courts increasingly expect statistical analysis (Benford's Law, anomaly detection) to support fraud claims. Most law firms lack the technical capability.
5. **Entity Relationships**: Kickback schemes involve complex webs of related parties. Mapping these relationships manually is error-prone and time-consuming.
6. **Case Economics**: The average FCA case takes 2-5 years. Attorneys working on contingency need efficiency to justify the investment. Faster case-building means more cases pursued and more fraud exposed.

## The Solution

ClaimForge is an AI-powered investigation platform purpose-built for False Claims Act and whistleblower cases. It transforms raw documents into structured, case-ready evidence.

### How It Works

1. **Ingest**: Upload invoices, contracts, emails, financial records, procurement documents (PDF, Excel, CSV, images). OCR handles scanned documents automatically.
2. **Analyze**: AI extracts entities (companies, people, amounts, dates), identifies relationships, and maps the flow of money.
3. **Detect**: Fraud detection models scan for known patterns — overbilling, duplicate billing, phantom vendors, quality substitution, kickbacks, bid rigging.
4. **Visualize**: Network graphs show entity relationships. Timelines show chronological fraud narratives. Statistical charts prove anomalies.
5. **Build**: Generate case-ready evidence packages with citations, chronological narratives, and FCA complaint drafts.

### Key Differentiators

- **FCA-Specific AI**: Not a general litigation tool. Every model, pattern, and workflow is tuned for False Claims Act cases.
- **Fraud Pattern Library**: Pre-built detection models for healthcare fraud (Medicare/Medicaid), defense contracting fraud (DCAA), pharmaceutical kickbacks, and more.
- **Case-Ready Output**: Evidence packages formatted for DOJ submission, complaint drafts aligned with FCA statutory requirements.
- **Statistical Rigor**: Built-in Benford's Law analysis, anomaly detection, clustering — the quantitative proof courts expect.
- **Security First**: End-to-end encryption, attorney-client privilege protections, SOC 2 compliance path, isolated data environments per case.
