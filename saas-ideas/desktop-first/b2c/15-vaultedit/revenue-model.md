### Pricing Rationale

**Free tier exists to:**
- Drive top-of-funnel acquisition (creators try VaultEdit risk-free)
- Generate word-of-mouth (even free users export videos with VaultEdit watermark = free advertising)
- Create upgrade pressure naturally (3-project limit and 720p cap are the primary conversion levers)
- Demonstrate value before asking for payment

**Creator tier at $19.99/mo because:**
- Below the psychological $20 threshold
- Cheaper than one hour of a freelance editor ($50-150/hour)
- Competitive with Descript ($24/mo) and less than Premiere Pro ($22.99/mo + learning curve)
- The value proposition is clear: save 6+ hours of editing per video = less than $5/hour of saved time at 1 video/week

**Pro tier at $39.99/mo because:**
- 2x Creator tier, unlocking team and business features
- Targets content teams and small agencies who need collaboration
- Brand kit and custom templates justify the premium for serious creators
- API access enables workflow automation for power users

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

```
LTV = ARPU x Gross Margin x Average Lifespan

ARPU         = $28.57/month (blended)
Gross Margin = 93% (API costs are ~7% of revenue)
Avg Lifespan = 20 months (video editors are sticky -- high switching costs)

LTV = $28.57 x 0.93 x 20 = $531.40

With expansion revenue (template purchases, team seat additions):
LTV = $571.00
```

### LTV:CAC Ratio

```
LTV:CAC = $571 / $25 = 22.8x

Industry benchmark: 3x is healthy, 5x+ is excellent
VaultEdit at 22.8x indicates room to increase CAC (spend more on acquisition)
```

### Payback Period

```
Payback Period = CAC / (ARPU x Gross Margin)
               = $25 / ($28.57 x 0.93)
               = $25 / $26.57
               = 0.94 months

Payback in under 1 month = extremely capital-efficient
```

## Acquisition Strategy

### Channel 1: YouTube Creator Tutorials (Primary)

**Strategy:** Create a VaultEdit YouTube channel that publishes editing tutorials demonstrating VaultEdit's speed advantage. Each video targets a specific creator pain point.

**Content Calendar:**

| Video Title (Example) | Target Keyword | Audience |
|---|---|---|
| "I edited a 20-min video in 15 minutes (here's how)" | "edit videos faster" | All creators |
| "Remove all ums and dead air with one click" | "remove silence from video" | Talking head creators |
| "Add captions to your YouTube video in 30 seconds" | "add captions youtube" | All creators |
| "Edit by reading: the future of video editing" | "transcript video editing" | Tech-savvy creators |
| "Turn your YouTube video into 5 TikToks automatically" | "repurpose youtube tiktok" | Multi-platform creators |

**Why This Works:**
- Creators search for editing help on YouTube -- meet them where they are
- Demo videos serve as both education and product marketing
- Evergreen content compounds over time (SEO)
- Each video can target a specific $19.99/mo converting pain point

**Investment:** 1 content creator/marketer (full-time), $2K/month production budget
**Expected CAC:** $15 (lowest of all channels)

### Channel 2: YouTube Ads Targeting Creators

**Strategy:** Run YouTube pre-roll and discovery ads targeting creator-related search terms and channels.

**Targeting:**

- Keywords: "how to edit youtube videos," "best video editing software," "video editing tutorial"
- Channels: creator education channels (Ali Abdaal, Thomas Frank, Film Riot)
- Custom intent: users who searched for "premiere pro alternative," "descript alternative," "capcut pro"
- Demographics: 18-44, interested in content creation, video editing, YouTube

**Budget:** $10K/month initially, scaling to $50K/month at profitability
**Expected CAC:** $25
**Expected CPA target:** $25 for free download, $45 for paid conversion (given 55% trial-to-paid)

### Channel 3: Creator Affiliate Partnerships

**Strategy:** Partner with mid-tier YouTube creators (50K-500K subscribers) who can authentically recommend VaultEdit in their videos.

**Partnership Structure:**

- 30% recurring commission on referred users for the first 3 months
- 15% recurring commission after month 3 (forever)
- Free Pro account for the partner
- Co-branded landing pages with the creator's name and content
- Custom discount codes (10% off first 3 months)

**Example Math:**
- Creator with 200K subscribers mentions VaultEdit in a video
- 50,000 views, 2% click-through = 1,000 visits
- 35% download = 350 downloads
- 1.3% visit-to-paid = 13 paying users
- Commission: 13 x $28.57 x 0.30 x 3 months = $334 to the creator
- Revenue: 13 x $28.57 x 12 months = $4,457 first-year revenue
- Net CAC: $334 / 13 = $25.69

### Channel 4: TikTok Demos

**Strategy:** Post short-form video demos showing VaultEdit's AI features in action. The "before/after" format performs extremely well on TikTok.

**Content Style:**
- "Watch me edit this entire video in 2 minutes" (speed demo)
- "This AI removed 47 seconds of dead air from my video" (result reveal)
- "One command. That's it. That's the edit." (AI command demo)

**Expected CAC:** $20 (organic + boosted posts)

### Channel 5: ProductHunt Launch

**Strategy:** Coordinate a major ProductHunt launch for v1.0 with community mobilization.

**Preparation:**
- Build a waitlist of 5,000+ people before launch
- Coordinate with 10+ creator partners to post about the launch simultaneously
- Prepare a 2-minute demo video showing the core workflow
- Offer "Founding Creator" lifetime discount for first 100 PH users (40% off annual, forever)

**Expected Result:** 2,000-5,000 downloads in the first week, 200-500 immediate paid conversions

### Channel 6: Creator Conferences

**Strategy:** Sponsor and demo at VidCon, Playlist Live, Creator Economy Expo, and Podcast Movement.

**Booth Strategy:**
- Live editing demo: bring your footage, leave with an edited video in 10 minutes
- Creator photo opportunity (builds social proof)
- Free sticker/merch with QR code to download
- "VaultEdit Challenge": edit a video faster than a professional editor using VaultEdit

**Budget:** $15K per conference (booth, travel, materials)
**Expected Leads:** 500-1,000 per conference
**Expected CAC:** $40 (higher but high-intent leads)

| **Blended** | **4.8%** | **44%** | **20 months** |

*Annual churn measured as non-renewal rate at the end of the annual period, expressed as equivalent monthly rate.

### Churn Reasons (Anticipated)

| Reason | % of Churns | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| "Not editing videos anymore" (quit creating) | 30% | Cannot prevent -- natural creator lifecycle |
| "Switched to a competitor" | 15% | Continuous feature development, AI advantage compounds |
| "Too expensive" | 20% | Annual discount nudges, demonstrate ROI in-app ("You saved 42 hours this month") |
| "Missing features I need" | 15% | Feature request tracking, public roadmap, fast iteration |
| "Bugs / performance issues" | 10% | Quality engineering, fast bug fix cycles, beta channel |
| "Outgrew VaultEdit (need full NLE)" | 10% | Add power-user features progressively, never ceiling creators |

### Churn Reduction Strategies

1. **In-app value reinforcement**: Show "You saved X hours this month" on the dashboard. Make the ROI visible.
2. **Annual plan incentives**: 20% discount + exclusive features for annual subscribers. Target 40% annual adoption.
3. **Switching cost accumulation**: Brand kits, custom templates, trained AI style preferences -- the longer you use VaultEdit, the more personalized it becomes. Switching means starting over.
4. **Community lock-in**: Template marketplace, shared presets, creator community. Social connections increase retention.
5. **Win-back campaigns**: When a user cancels, offer 2 months free to return. 15-20% win-back rate expected.
6. **Pause instead of cancel**: Offer a "pause subscription" option (keep projects, keep settings, just pause billing for up to 3 months). Reduces permanent churn by 10-15%.

### Price Justification vs. Competitors

**vs. Premiere Pro ($22.99/mo):** VaultEdit is $3 cheaper AND saves 6+ hours per video. Premiere requires months to learn; VaultEdit works in minutes. The value gap is enormous.

**vs. Descript ($24/mo Creator tier):** VaultEdit is $4 cheaper with more YouTube-specific features (chapters, thumbnails, retention optimization). Descript is increasingly podcast-focused.

**vs. CapCut Pro ($9.99/mo):** VaultEdit is 2x the price but offers professional-grade AI editing that CapCut does not. Different target: CapCut is for casual social video, VaultEdit is for serious YouTube creators.

**vs. DaVinci Resolve (Free):** DaVinci is free but has a 6-month learning curve. VaultEdit's value is time saved, not feature access. A creator earning $5,000/month who saves 30 hours/month of editing is saving $150/hour equivalent -- $19.99/mo is a rounding error.
