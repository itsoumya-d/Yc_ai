# SpectraCAD --- Revenue Model

---

## Pricing Strategy

SpectraCAD uses a freemium model with tiered subscriptions designed to capture users at every stage of their hardware journey --- from hobbyist prototyper to enterprise engineering team. The free tier is generous enough to build a real product (2-layer boards), creating organic word-of-mouth, while premium tiers unlock the AI power, collaboration, and manufacturing integrations that professional teams need.

## Expansion Revenue

Beyond core subscriptions, SpectraCAD has multiple expansion revenue vectors.

### Revenue Stream 1: Component Marketplace Commissions

| Metric                     | Value                                               |
| -------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **Model**                  | Affiliate commission on component orders via BOM    |
| **Commission rate**        | 2--5% of component order value                     |
| **Average BOM per board**  | $15--$200                                          |
| **Average orders/user/yr** | 3--12                                              |
| **Revenue per user/yr**    | $2--$50 (incremental)                              |
| **At 10K users**           | $50K--$200K/yr additional revenue                  |

When a user orders components through SpectraCAD's BOM manager (deep link to DigiKey/Mouser cart), SpectraCAD earns an affiliate commission. This is transparent to the user and does not increase their cost.

## Pricing Philosophy

1. **Value-based, not cost-based:** SpectraCAD saves hardware startups $5K--$15K per board revision. A $49--$149/mo subscription is trivially justified against this saving.

2. **Land and expand:** Free tier gets the tool on every hardware engineer's desktop. AI features and collaboration drive upgrades. Enterprise compliance locks in large accounts.

3. **Transparent pricing:** No hidden fees, no per-export charges, no per-board charges. Simple per-seat pricing that scales predictably for customers.

4. **Annual incentives:** 20% discount for annual billing improves cash flow and reduces churn.

5. **No feature crippling:** Free tier produces real, manufacturable Gerber files. We never hold essential output hostage --- that builds resentment, not loyalty.

---

*Revenue follows value. SpectraCAD's revenue model works because each tier delivers clear, measurable value: time saved, errors avoided, costs reduced, and boards shipped.*
